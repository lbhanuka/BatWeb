What is our goal?

Our goal is to collect bat sightings and display the accurate distribution of bats in Sri Lanka.

What are our objectives?

  • To collect bat sightings of Sri Lanka
  • To collect research documents related to bats
  • To display the overall distribution of bats in Sri Lanka
  • To give an opportunity of clarifying doubts regarding bats
