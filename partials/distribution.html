Help to view the distribution of bat species

Here you can view the distribution of bat species by customizing according to your preference. At the beginning you will see a list of species and you will see the distribution of all species on the Sri Lankan map.

If you want to filter and see only some of the species, then you can click the Hide all button and then turn on the toggle switch button infront of each species. If it is on then you can see it in Green color.

 If you want to get the information of each sighting which are on the map, Then you have to click a particular sighting point on the map. Then you will see the sighting details using a pop up window. eg: date, species, count,etc
